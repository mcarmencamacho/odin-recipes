KASHIWA-MESHI

Servings

This recipe can make about 4 servings. Take into account this is not a main dish, but side dish to eat with a main one.

What do you need?

Ingredients Quantity
Rice 255g
Chicken breast 120g
Burdock root 60g
Carrots 60g
Dried shiitake mushrooms 4 small mushrooms
Sesame oil 5 ml
Soy sauce 20ml
Sake 20ml
Mirin 20ml
Sugar 20g
Scallions 12g

How to prepare it?

  1. Get those ingredients ready!
    1. Wash and rinse the rice and let it sit for 30-60min.
    2. Cut the chicken into small pieces.
    3. Cut the carrots and burdock into strips.
    4. Rehydrate the mushrooms and cut them in half.
  2. Let's cook!
    1. Heat a pan on medium heat, add sesame oil and lightly stir-fry all the cut ingredients.
    2. when they are cooked add the seasonings, mix well and saute on medium heat.
    3. When the liquid is reduced by half, turn off the heat and let it cool.
    4. Cook the rice in another pot with some dashi.
  3. Ready to eat!
    1. Remove the rice from the heat and mix well with the ingredients and soup.
    2. Garnish with some scallions.
    3. Enjoy!
