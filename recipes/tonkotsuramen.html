TONKOTSU RAMEN

Servings

This recipe can make about 4 servings.
This is a very filling main dish,
but feel free to ea it with some gyoza or kashiwa meshi!

What do you need?

Ingredients Quantity
Pork broth 2l
Ramen noodles 400g
Soft-boiled eggs 4 large eggs
Ear mushrooms 80g
Chashu braised pork 12 slices (25g each approx.)
Garlic 6 cloves
Soy sauce 75ml
Mirin 30ml
Sake 30ml
Scallions 12g
Toasted sesame seeds 12g
Red pickled ginger 40g

How to prepare it?

  1. Getting the seasoning ready
    1. Peel the garlic cloves and crush with the flat part of a knife.
    2. Place the garlic, soy sauce, mirin and sake in a small sauce pan. bring to a boil and then reduce and simmer for 10 minutes.

  2. Let's cook!
    1. Boil the noodles in water about 4 minutes. No salt needed.
    2. Cook the mushrooms along side the noodles until they are soften.
    3. Slightly fry the braised pork in a on-tick pan until lightly browned.

  3. Ready to eat!
    1. Place 1/4 of the seasoning in the bottom of four bowls.
    2. Pour about 125ml of broth into each bowl and stir to mix.
    3. Add the noodles. Pour another 375ml of broth per bowl.
    4. Top each bowl with one egg, about 2-3 braised pork slices, and about 20g of mushrooms.
    5. Additionally, garnish with sesame seeds and scallions.
    6. Enjoy!
