CHIKUZEN-NI

Servings

This recipe can make about 4 or 5 servings.
Take into account this is not a main dish,
but something to be shared together with more food.

What do you need?

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken thigh 250-300g
Carrots 80g
Dried shiitake mushrooms 10g (3-4 pieces)
Konjac 100g
Potatoes (or, ideally, taro) 300g
Burdock root 100g
Green beans 20g
Dashi soup stock 200ml (2 cups approx.)
Soy sauce 25-30ml
Salt 4-5g
Mirin 30ml
Sugar 20-30g
Oil 1 to 2 tbsp
Grated ginger 15g

How to prepare it?

  1. Getting the ingredients ready
    1. Cut chicken and konjac into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Cut potatoes, carrots and burdock roots into small chunks.
    3. Cut konjac into reins.
    4. Rehydrate the mushrooms and cut them in half.
    5. Prepare the seasoning by mixing together soy sauce, mirin, salt and sugar.
    6. Boil the green beans in salted water and cut into pieces of 4-5cm.

  2. Let's cook!
    1. Heat the oil in a pan and lightly stir-fry the chicken.
    2. Add 2-3 mushroom pieces and stir-fry a bit more.
    3. Add the dashi stock and the seasoning. Let it simmer until everything is cooked through and the broth evaporates.

  3. Ready to eat!
    1. Remove from the heat, cover and let it cool.
    2. Serve in a bowl and garnish on top with the ginger and green beans.
    3. Enjoy!
